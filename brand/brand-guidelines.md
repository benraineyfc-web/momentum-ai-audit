## 1. Color System

**Primary Color: Deep Blue (#1E40AF)**
- Use for: Main CTAs, headings, primary UI elements
- Conveys: Confidence, trust, professionalism
- Accessibility: WCAG AA compliant with white text

**Secondary Color: Vibrant Teal (#06B6D4)**
- Use for: Accents, highlights, gradients
- Conveys: Energy, forward momentum, innovation
- Pairs well with primary for gradients

**Accent Color: Trust Green (#10B981)**
- Use for: Success states, positive indicators, checkmarks
- Conveys: Results, growth, validation
- Use sparingly for emphasis

**Neutral Scale:**
- 900 (#111827): Body text, headings
- 700 (#374151): Secondary text
- 500 (#6B7280): Muted text, captions
- 300 (#D1D5DB): Borders, dividers
- 100 (#F3F4F6): Backgrounds, subtle fills

**State Colors:**
- Success: #10B981 (green)
- Warning: #F59E0B (orange)
- Error: #EF4444 (red)

## 3. Logo System

**Primary Logo:** `/brand/logos/momentum-logo.svg`

**Logo Variations:**
1. **Full Logo** (icon + wordmark): Use on landing page header, email signatures
2. **Icon Only**: Use for favicons, app icons, social media avatars
3. **Reversed** (white on dark): Use on dark backgrounds (hero sections, footers)
4. **Monochrome**: Use when color printing is not available

**Logo Usage Rules:**
- Minimum size: 120px width (full logo), 32px (icon only)
- Clear space: Minimum 16px padding on all sides
- DO NOT: Stretch, rotate, add effects, change colors outside brand palette
- Backgrounds: Use on white, neutral-50, or brand colors only

**Icon Design:**
- Abstract forward arrows symbolizing momentum and progress
- Two overlapping arrows create depth and movement
- Primary blue (#1E40AF) + secondary teal (#06B6D4) with opacity

**Wordmark:**
- Font: Inter Bold (700)
- All lowercase: "momentum"
- Color: Neutral 900 (#111827) or white (reversed)

## 4. Voice & Tone Guide

### Brand Voice Principles

**1. Founder-Led & Direct**
- Write like you're talking to a mate over coffee
- No corporate speak, no jargon unless necessary
- Use "I" (Ben's voice), not "we" or "our team"

**2. No-BS, Results-Focused**
- Say what you mean: "Here's what you get" not "Unlock your potential"
- Avoid guru culture, hype, and empty promises
- Lead with tangible outcomes: time saved, money saved, revenue gained

**3. Relatable & Human**
- Acknowledge the overwhelm: "AI feels like a lot. It doesn't have to be."
- No gatekeeping: "You don't need a tech degree for this"
- British humour where appropriate (dry, self-aware, not forced)

**4. British English, UK Market**
- Spelling: Colour, optimise, analyse, centre
- Currency: ¬£ (GBP), not $
- Cultural references: UK-specific (plumbers, care homes, local businesses)

## 6. Design Principles

**1. Clean & Minimal**
- White space is your friend
- Don't clutter layouts
- One clear action per section

**2. Mobile-First**
- 70% of traffic is mobile
- Design for small screens first
- Touch-friendly CTAs (min 44px height)

**3. Accessibility**
- WCAG AA compliance minimum
- High contrast text (4.5:1 ratio)
- Keyboard navigation support
- Screen reader friendly

**4. Performance**
- Fast load times (< 3s)
- Optimized images (WebP format)
- Minimal JavaScript

## Summary Checklist

When creating any Momentum asset, ask:

- [ ] Is the voice direct and founder-led? (No "we", use "I")
- [ ] Does it lead with the pain/problem?
- [ ] Is pricing transparent? (¬£497, not hidden)
- [ ] Is it UK-focused? (British English, ¬£, local examples)
- [ ] Does it avoid jargon and hype?
- [ ] Are colors from the brand palette?
- [ ] Is typography Inter (headings + body)?
- [ ] Is the logo used correctly?

- [ ] Is the CTA clear and specific?

**If you answer "no" to any of these, revise before publishing.**
