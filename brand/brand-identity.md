## Core Messaging Pillars

### Pillar 1: Outcomes Over Technology
Business owners don't care about AI. They care about saving 20 hours a week, cutting costs by 30%, or generating leads while they sleep. We lead with the result, not the tool.

**Key Messages:**
- "Save time. Cut costs. Grow revenue. That's it."
- "You don't need to understand AI. You just need it working for you."
- "We speak business, not tech jargon."

### Pillar 2: Done-For-You, Not DIY
Our target market doesn't want to learn n8n or build Zapier workflows. They want someone to do it and show them the results.

**Key Messages:**
- "We build it. You benefit."
- "No courses. No tutorials. No DIY. Just results."
- "Your AI department, without hiring one."

### Pillar 3: Proof Over Promises
Every claim backed by numbers. Every service starts with a diagnostic. No mystery, no vague "transformation" language.

**Key Messages:**
- "We show you exactly what's possible before you spend a penny on implementation."
- "Every recommendation comes with an ROI estimate."
- "Free audit. Real numbers. Your decision."

### Pillar 4: Accessible Entry, Scalable Growth
Start with a free or low-cost audit. Scale to full automation builds and ongoing AI leadership. No pressure, just a ladder.

**Key Messages:**
- "Start with a scan. Scale when you're ready."
- "From audit to automation to full AI strategy."
- "No lock-in. No retainer pressure. Just results."

- Red: #EF4444 (errors, urgent)

### Typography

**Headings:** Inter (Bold/ExtraBold, 700/800)
- H1: 48-64px / tracking tight
- H2: 36-48px / tracking tight
- H3: 24-30px / tracking normal

**Body:** Inter (Regular/Medium, 400/500)
- Body: 16-18px / leading relaxed (1.6-1.75)
- Small: 14px / leading normal

**Mono (code/technical):** JetBrains Mono
- For technical contexts, pricing, stats

**Why Inter:** Free (Google Fonts), excellent readability, works at all sizes, clean geometric feel, widely supported. Used by Linear, Vercel, and other modern SaaS brands.
